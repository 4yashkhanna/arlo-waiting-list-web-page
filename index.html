Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before joining the list.

What is Arlo?
A personal knowledge library for your iPhone. Save any link, video, reel, or screenshot, and Arlo's AI reads it, organizes it, and makes it searchable, so the things you save are actually useful later.
What can I save?
YouTube videos, Instagram reels, TikToks, articles, web pages, photos, and screenshots, straight from the iOS share sheet or inside the app. Arlo pulls out the specific details automatically.
How is it different from just bookmarking?
Bookmarks pile up and get forgotten. Arlo understands what you saved, lets you search by meaning, chat with your library, and resurfaces things at the right moment, without you having to remember to look.
Does it work with Claude and ChatGPT?
Yes. Connect Arlo once via MCP and your entire library becomes available inside Claude and ChatGPT. Ask a question in your AI chat and it'll quietly check your saves first and surface anything relevant.
Is my library private?
Yes. Your saves are yours, used only to power your own search, chat, and recall. Nothing is shared or used to train models.
What does early access get me?
First access before public launch, direct input into what Arlo builds next, and a way to shape the product while it's still early enough to matter.
