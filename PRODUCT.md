# Product: Arlo Waitlist Landing Page

## Register
brand

## Purpose
Waitlist capture page for Arlo — a personal AI knowledge library for iPhone. Saves links, videos, articles, reels, and screenshots; reads and organises them automatically; resurfaces them inside Claude and ChatGPT via MCP.

## Target users
Design-conscious early adopters in their 20s–30s: students, creators, founders, professionals who save things constantly and lose them just as fast.

## Desired outcome
Email signup on the waitlist form. Secondary: survey completion to shape the roadmap.

## Brand personality
Warm, intellectual, personal. Not clinical. Not hype-y. The product is about knowledge that actually stays with you — calm confidence, not startup urgency.

## Anti-references
- Generic second-brain / notes-app aesthetic (Lora + warm cream + terracotta = the category reflex)
- Notion-adjacent editorial layouts
- AI-purple gradient anything

## Design principles
1. Glass surfaces over flat cards — the page has a frosted, layered depth
2. Bricolage Grotesque (display) + Figtree (body) — warm grotesque, not the Lora/Inter default
3. Terracotta accent (#944521) + sage secondary (#526254) — carried by accent, not the background
4. Motion is intentional: hover lifts, button press feedback, FAQ accordion, scroll reveal
5. No eyebrows on every section — used sparingly as brand voice

## Tech
Single self-contained HTML file. No framework. CSS custom properties for tokens. Vanilla JS for the survey, FAQ, and form.
